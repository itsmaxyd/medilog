Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

MediLog is built with a simple philosophy: your medical data belongs to you. Since the app is completely offline, we have no access to your information.

1. Information Collection

MediLog does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. All information you enter‚Äîincluding medical records, profiles, and health data‚Äîis stored locally on your device's internal storage.

2. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions only to enable specific features:

  • Camera: Used solely for OCR scanning of medical documents.
  • Storage: Used to save and retrieve your medical records locally.

These permissions are used purely on-device and no data is shared with the developer or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

MediLog does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or cloud synchronization services. It is a completely self-contained application.

4. Data Security

Because your data is stored on your device, its security depends on the security of your physical device. We recommend using device encryption and secure screen locks.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via our GitHub repository.
