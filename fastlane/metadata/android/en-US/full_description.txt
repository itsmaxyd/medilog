MediLog is a complete, offline-first solution for managing your family's medical history.

Features:
- Privacy First: 100% offline, no cloud sync, no tracking
- Multiple Profiles: Manage records for the whole family
- Medicine Database: Offline access to 253,000+ medicines
- Doctor Visits: Record prescriptions, tests, and follow-ups
- Health Tracking: Monitor BP, weight, and other vitals
- Smart Reminders: Medicine schedule and follow-up alerts
- OCR Scanning: Scan prescriptions and reports
- Elderly Friendly: Large text and simple navigation

Open Source and F-Droid compliant.
